Introduction

Skyzen Media LLC ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By accessing or using our services, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access our services.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Fill out our contact form
  • Subscribe to our newsletter
  • Opt-in to receive SMS/text message communications
  • Request information about our products or services
  • Communicate with us via email, phone, or other means

This information may include:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (including mobile phone number for SMS communications)
  • Company name
  • Any other information you choose to provide

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain information about your device, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referring website addresses
  • Pages viewed and time spent on pages
  • Date and time of visits

How We Use Your Information

We may use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • To send you updates about our products and services
  • To improve our website and services
  • To analyze usage patterns and trends
  • To protect against fraudulent or unauthorized activity
  • To comply with legal obligations

SMS/Text Message Communications

If you opt-in to receive SMS/text message communications from Skyzen Media LLC, we collect your mobile phone number for the purpose of sending you text messages related to our services, updates, alerts, and other communications you have consented to receive.

Important: We do not sell, rent, share, or trade your mobile phone number or any personal information collected in connection with our SMS program to third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Your phone number will only be used to send you the messages you have opted-in to receive.

Message frequency may vary. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt-out of receiving SMS messages at any time by replying "STOP" to any message you receive from us. After opting out, you will receive a one-time confirmation message, and no further messages will be sent unless you re-subscribe.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information, including your mobile phone number, to third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website and conducting our business, subject to confidentiality agreements. These providers are prohibited from using your information for their own marketing purposes.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information when required by law or to protect our rights, property, or safety.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity, who will be bound by similar privacy protections.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website. Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help us understand how you use our site. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • The right to access your personal information
  • The right to correct inaccurate information
  • The right to delete your personal information
  • The right to opt-out of marketing communications

Opting Out of SMS Communications

You can stop receiving SMS messages from us at any time by:

  • Replying "STOP" to any SMS message you receive from us
  • Contacting us directly using the information provided below

After you opt-out, you will receive a final confirmation message. Please note that opting out of SMS messages will not affect other communications you may receive from us, such as email.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.
